ESG Assistant — Sustainability Reporting Platform for SMEs

Project Information

  • Project Type: SaaS Platform
  • Role: Full-Stack Architect & Product Designer
  • Status: In Development
  • Tech Stack: Next.js 14, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, Supabase, PostgreSQL, Prisma, Stripe, AI (GPT-5)

Overview

ESG Assistant helps small and mid-sized businesses calculate emissions, generate compliance-ready ESG reports, and receive guided sustainability recommendations — without hiring consultants or managing complex spreadsheets.

Key Features

  • AI-powered emissions calculation (Scope 1, 2 & 3).
  • CSV/Excel upload with auto-mapping and validation.
  • Forecasting and “what-if” simulation tools.
  • AI-generated ESG reporting aligned with frameworks.
  • In-app ESG assistant chatbot for support and explanations.

Design Goals

  • Provide a simple, step-by-step guided process instead of overwhelming dashboards.
  • Make sustainability approachable for non-experts.
  • Ensure transparency and editable AI-generated insights.

Current Progress

The platform currently includes onboarding, data importing, dashboard scaffolding, authentication, and billing logic. Upcoming milestones include deeper AI insights, forecasting engine, and full reporting module.
