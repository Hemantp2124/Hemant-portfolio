ProofCraft – AI Compliance & Launch Readiness Platform

Project Information

  • Project Type: SaaS Compliance Platform
  • Role: Product Architect, Full-Stack Engineer & UX Designer
  • Status: Phase 2 Completed — Fully Interactive Prototype
  • Tech Stack: Next.js 14, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, Shadcn UI, Zustand, Supabase, Stripe, Clerk/Auth.js, AI Router (GPT + Claude + Llama via OpenRouter)

Overview

ProofCraft is an AI-powered compliance system that helps SaaS founders, agencies, and eCommerce brands scan websites and applications for GDPR, cookie, accessibility (WCAG), and legal policy violations. The platform generates fixes, remediation guidance, compliance reports, and draft legal documents—all before launch.

Key Features

  • AI-driven compliance scanning with prioritized issue detection.
  • Fix Assistant with actionable remediation recommendations.
  • Automated legal document drafting (Privacy Policy, ToS, Cookie Notice, DPA).
  • Compliance reports with severity scoring, summaries, and risk heatmaps.
  • Future: Monitoring engine with scheduled scans & platform webhooks.

Design Goals

  • Simplify complex compliance workflows through conversational AI UX.
  • Build a calming, trustworthy UI with predictable navigation and actions.
  • Create a modular component system supporting future enterprise features.

Current Progress

The platform has completed Phase 2 with fully functional user flows, mock intelligence, dynamic UI, legal document previews, persistent settings, and report generation. Phase 3 will integrate real compliance intelligence powered by GPT-5.1, Claude, OpenRouter, and LLaMA models to produce actionable, accurate compliance outcomes and fully automated legal reporting.
