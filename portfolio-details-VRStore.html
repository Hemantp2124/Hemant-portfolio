VR_STORE

Project information :

Overview :

VR Grocery Haven redefines shopping with a groundbreaking concept in virtual reality, offering an immersive experience amidst the convenience of your home. Our vision blends seamless convenience with delightful exploration, catering to discerning shoppers seeking unparalleled ease and quality.

Objectives of Research:

  • Evaluate customer satisfaction levels with VR shopping experience through feedback and surveys.
  • Identify areas of improvement in VR technology usability, interface design, and overall user experience.
  • Understand consumer preferences and behaviors in virtual reality shopping environments.
  • Develop personalized marketing strategies based on consumer demographics and shopping habits in VR.

    • Analyze competitors' approaches in virtual environment design, product presentation, and user interface to identify areas for superior user experience. Emphasize intuitive and visually appealing virtual environments to elevate VR Store's overall shopping experience. Implement user-centric design principles to streamline navigation and interaction across virtual and digital platforms
