Shopping App UI Design

Project information :

Overview :

Mobile technology has changed the way we live, especially under the pandemic situation. As a result, mobile shopping trends are evolving rapidly during the year. Consumer buying behaviour has changed significantly because of the work from home culture, and it will continue to grow in the future.

Objectives of research :

  • The app design brings the users a simple, smooth shopping experience in the process of getting their hands on all the freshest drops.
  • Designed for modern clothing store with shopping features like visual search, lookbooks, fashion blogs, collections, etc.
  • The clean and solid style creates a user-friendly and aesthetic app that improves the user’s experience in online shopping.

    • To know what users like and what they still wish to have in these existing applications, I used other shopping apps. After seeing the interface of such apps I realise that there are many things we can design in better way like colour combinations, the overall layout of the screen, quick friendly interface these are the basic sides I have considered while desighning. The main objective for desighning shopping apps is to attract and impress the consumers.
