Music List UI Design

Project information :

Overview :

Music has influenced and motivated people’s mind and heart since ages and still it continues to do so. But now, with digital and advanced technologies around us, Music plays one of the important role in one’s life rather than just being a source of entertainment.

Objectives of research :

  • To provide efficient switching to next song or other playlists.
  • To keep users updated and informed about all the music related happenings around them.
  • To be able to carry out multiple actions any user wishes to have in one app.

    • To know what users like and what they still wish to have in these existing applications, I used other music apps. After seeing the interface of such apps I realise that there are many things we can design in better way like colour combinations, the overall layout of the screen, quick friendly interface these are the basic sides I have considered while desighning.
